# 🌾 Zindagiasaan: Agri-Trade Intelligence Agent

> **Bridging the gap between Pakistani Horticulture and Global Markets.**

Zindagiasaan is a specialized AI agent designed for **Farmer Enterprise Groups (FEGs)** and exporters in Pakistan. It automates the complex task of cross-referencing local regulations (**TIPP**) with international market demand (**ITC**).

## ✨ Core Features
* **Real-Time TIPP Analysis:** Direct extraction of procedures from Procedure ID 305 (Vegetable/Fruit Exports).
* **ITC Market Insights:** Identifies untapped export potential in the EU and Gulf markets.
* **Waste-to-Wealth Engine:** Strategic ROI analysis for transitioning from fresh exports to **Chapter 20 (Freeze-Drying)**.
* **Bilingual Support:** Generates professional English strategies and rural-friendly Urdu reports.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack
* **Engine:** Gemini 3.1 (Flash)
* **Interface:** Streamlit
* **Data Sources:** TIPP (Trade Information Portal of Pakistan) & ITC Market Access Map.
